About Me
Game Design is my passion

My name is Emmanuel Mukwakungu,I am an aspiring Game Designer, Game Developer, and Web Designer based in Cape Town, South Africa. I hold qualifications in Game Design from SAE Institute and IT from Varsity College, where I developed a strong foundation in both the creative and technical sides of digital media. My journey into game design started with a simple passion — I loved games and wanted to understand how they worked. That curiosity pushed me to learn and study Unity, C#, and the principles behind game mechanics, level design, and player experience. Over time I've built projects ranging from fast-paced arcade games to story-driven concepts, each one teaching me something new about what makes a game feel good to play. Outside of games, I enjoy web design — there's something satisfying about building something clean and functional from scratch using just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I see it as another form of design thinking: understanding what a user needs and crafting an experience around that. When I'm not at a screen, you'll find me on the basketball court. The game has taught me a lot about teamwork, focus, and performing under pressure — qualities I carry into everything I do. I'm currently seeking opportunities where I can contribute my skills, keep growing, and work on projects that matter. If you have something in mind, I'd love to hear from it.
